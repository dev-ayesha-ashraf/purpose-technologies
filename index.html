Frequently Asked Questions
How Does Purpose Technologies Work?
Purpose Technologies' all-in-one payment platform enables businesses to manage online payments and disbursements with ease. Through one integrated dashboard, companies can access a unified view of balances across collections and payouts, covering various regions and currencies. Our platform is built for flexibility, helping businesses gain full control of their financial operations. Additionally, our built-in authentication, identity verification, and fraud detection tools ensure a secure transaction environment, empowering businesses to confidently accept legitimate payments while minimizing risk.
Is Purpose Technologies easy to integrate with?
Yes! Purpose Technologies offers multiple integration options to suit different business needs. Our hosted payment pages enable businesses to collect payment information securely and redirect customers to a safe checkout experience. Integration is made easy with well-documented APIs, clear technical guidelines, and a comprehensive FAQ repository. Our dedicated support team is also available to assist throughout the integration journey.
Which payment methods does Purpose Technologies support?
Purpose Technologies supports a wide range of payment methods including major card schemes, direct debits, vouchers, digital wallets, and bank transfers. We focus on offering localized and consumer-friendly solutions — whether through secure digital wallets, regional voucher systems, or traditional banking rails.

FAQ

Questions we get asked

How long does a typical web or app project take?

A Starter Website takes 2–4 weeks. A Business Platform 6–10 weeks. An Enterprise or SaaS project is 12–24 weeks depending on scope. Mobile app timelines are similar. We provide detailed project timelines during the scoping call before any commitment is made.

Can I use Settlix without building my project with Purpose Technologies?

Yes. Settlix is available as a standalone product with its own API and documentation. Any developer can integrate Settlix into any existing platform. Clients who also use our development services receive Settlix at preferential commercial rates.

What is included in post-launch support?

Post-launch support covers bug fixes, minor content updates, performance monitoring, and technical assistance. The scope and duration depends on your package. For ongoing feature development, we offer monthly retainer arrangements that suit most growing businesses.

Are the prices on your pricing page fixed?

The prices shown are starting rates for the described scope. Every project is scoped and quoted individually after a short discovery call. We provide a detailed, fixed-price proposal — no hourly billing, no surprise invoices.

Do you work with clients outside Pakistan?

Yes. We work with clients in the UAE, UK, Saudi Arabia, and other markets — particularly for clients with Pakistan-facing digital operations or who want to leverage Pakistani talent and cost efficiency for their digital build.

Is Settlix regulated in Pakistan?

Settlix is operated by Purpose Technologies (Pvt) Ltd, registered with SECP. The platform is PCI DSS certified and implements AML/KYC controls on all transactions. For specific regulatory queries, please contact our team directly.
